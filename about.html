What We Do

Door Repair & Maintenance

We handle a variety of door-related issues including hinge problems, alignment concerns, hardware replacement, and lock installations. Our work covers entry doors, interior doors, and sliding door systems.

Window Repair Services

Our window services include frame repairs, glass replacement, seal restoration, and hardware fixes. We work with common residential window types to address issues that affect function and efficiency.

Hardware & Weather Sealing

We replace worn hardware components and install weather sealing to improve energy efficiency and comfort. This includes door handles, locks, screens, weather stripping, and door sweeps.
